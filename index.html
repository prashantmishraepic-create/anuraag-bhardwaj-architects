Common Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What architectural services do you provide?

+
We offer comprehensive architectural services including building design, interior design, Vastu consultancy, 3D visualization, renovation, construction management, and sustainable design solutions. Our expertise covers residential, commercial, and institutional projects across Delhi NCR and beyond.

How does Vastu consultancy work with modern architecture?

+
We seamlessly integrate Vastu Shastra principles with contemporary architectural design. Our approach ensures your space is not only aesthetically modern and functional but also aligned with traditional energy principles for enhanced well-being, prosperity, and harmony. We provide practical Vastu solutions without compromising on design aesthetics.

What is the typical project timeline?

+
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Typically, design phase takes 3-6 weeks, approvals 2-4 weeks, and construction 3-12 months depending on project size. We provide detailed timelines during consultation and maintain transparent communication throughout to ensure timely delivery.

Do you handle building approvals and permissions?

+
Yes, we manage all building approvals, permissions, and documentation required for your project. This includes municipal approvals, structural plans, fire safety clearances, and environmental compliance. Our experience with local authorities ensures smooth processing of all necessary permits.

What areas do you serve?

+
We are based in Vivek Vihar, Delhi, and primarily serve Delhi NCR including Noida, Gurgaon, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad. We also take on select projects across North India. For projects outside Delhi NCR, we assess feasibility during initial consultation.

How do I start a project with you?

+
Starting a project is simple: Contact us via phone, WhatsApp, or the contact form. We'll schedule an initial consultation to understand your requirements, budget, and timeline. After site visit and discussion, we'll provide a detailed proposal with design approach, timeline, and cost estimates. Once approved, we begin the design phase.
